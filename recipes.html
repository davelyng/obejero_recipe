Okonomiyaki

A Japanese savory pancake with a variety of flavors and textures, often served with okonomiyaki sauce, mayonnaise, and bonito flakes.

Ingredients:

  • All-purpose flour
  • Salt
  • Sugar
  • Baking powder
  • Grated mountain yam
  • Dashi (Japanese soup stock)
  • Eggs
  • Tenkasu (tempura scraps)
  • Pickled red ginger (kizami shoga)
  • Green cabbage
  • Sliced pork belly

Instructions:

  1. Combine all-purpose flour, salt, sugar, baking powder, grated mountain yam, and dashi in a bowl to make the base batter.
  2. Add eggs and mix well.
  3. Stir in tenkasu, pickled red ginger, and shredded cabbage.
  4. Heat a non-stick pan or griddle over medium heat.
  5. Pour a ladleful of batter onto the pan.
  6. Add sliced pork belly on top if desired.
  7. Cook until the bottom is golden brown, then flip and cook the other side.
  8. Serve hot with okonomiyaki sauce, mayonnaise, and bonito flakes.

Tips and Techniques:

  • Substitute grated regular yam if grated mountain yam is unavailable.
  • Vegan dashi can be made using kombu (dried kelp) and dried shiitake mushrooms.
  • Customize toppings according to preference, such as seafood, cheese, or vegetables.

Lasagna

An Italian dish with a layers of pasta, rich tomato sauce, creamy béchamel, and cheese, baked until golden and bubbly.

Ingredients:

  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Ground beef chuck
  • Onion
  • Bell pepper
  • Garlic
  • Tomato sauce
  • Tomato paste
  • Crushed tomatoes
  • Fresh oregano
  • Fresh parsley
  • Italian seasoning
  • Garlic powder and/or garlic salt
  • Red or white wine vinegar
  • Sugarr
  • Salt
  • Dry lasagna noodles
  • Cheese

Instructions:

  1. Saute ground beef, onions, bell peppers, and garlic.
  2. Stir in tomato sauce, tomato paste, crushed tomatoes, and seasonings. Simmer.
  3. Cook lasagna noodles and drain.
  4. Mix cheese with parsley.
  5. Preheat oven. Assemble layers. Repeat.
  6. Finish with meat sauce and cheese.
  7. Bake covered, then uncovered.
  8. Let it rest before serving.

Tips and Techniques:

  • Use no-boil lasagna noodles for convenience.
  • Substitute ground turkey or Italian sausage for beef.
  • Make-ahead and refrigerate lasagna before baking.

Chicken Beef Taco

A seasoned ground beef and chicken, wrapped in a crispy taco shell. It is often garnished with fresh toppings like lettuce, tomatoes, cheese, and salsa

Ingredients:

  • Lean ground beef
  • Canned tomato sauce
  • Taco spice mix
  • Onion
  • Chicken breasts
  • Lime
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil

Instructions:

  1. For beef tacos, brown lean ground beef and sauté diced onions.
  2. Stir in canned tomato sauce and taco spice mix. Simmer.
  3. For chicken tacos, marinate chicken breasts in lime juice, crushed garlic, olive oil, and taco spice mix.
  4. Grill or pan-fry chicken until cooked through.
  5. Slice chicken and assemble tacos with desired toppings.
  6. Serve with tortillas.

Tips and Techniques:

  • Adjust the amount of tomato sauce for desired sauciness in beef tacos.
  • Marinate chicken for enhanced flavor.
  • Experiment with various taco toppings like lettuce, tomatoes, cheese, and salsa.

Meatballs

Flavorful and tender meatballs. It is often made with a mixture of ground meats, breadcrumbs, and seasonings, served in various dishes or as appetizers with sauce.

Ingredients:

  • Plain breadcrumbs
  • Grated Parmesan
  • Garlic powder
  • Onion powder
  • Italian seasoning
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Eggs
  • Whole milk
  • Italian sausage
  • Ground beef

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven. Combine dry ingredients.
  2. Whisk eggs and milk. Add to dry ingredients.
  3. Mix in Italian sausage and ground beef.
  4. Shape into meatballs and place on a baking sheet.
  5. Bake until cooked through.
  6. Serve with marinara sauce or as desired.

Tips and Techniques:

  • Brown meatballs for extra flavor.
  • Freeze leftover meatballs.
  • Customize seasoning to taste.

Beef Soup

A soup made with tender beef, potatoes, onions, and vegetables, simmered in a flavorful broth and seasoned with herbs and spices.

Ingredients:

  • Yellow onion
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Ground beef
  • Russet potato
  • Canned diced tomatoes
  • Italian seasoning
  • Black pepper
  • Beef broth
  • Worcestershire sauce
  • Frozen vegetables
  • Salt

Instructions:

  1. Saute onion and garlic.
  2. Add ground beef and brown.
  3. Stir in potatoes, tomatoes, seasonings, broth, and Worcestershire sauce. Simmer.
  4. Add frozen vegetables. Simmer.
  5. Season to taste.

Tips and Techniques:

  • Add wine or beef bouillon for richer flavor.
  • Freeze leftovers.
